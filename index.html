Jiahe Xu

I am a Robotics System Engineer(Sensor, System & Software) at CMU. My research focus on Object-Centric Manipulation, Bimanual Manipulation, Learning from Human Video, 3D Robot Learning. I hope to build intelligent systems that can be applied to any robot with a handful number of demonstrations. My past experiences includes sensor calibration(Pinhole, Fisheye, Stereo, Thermal cameras, IMU and Lidar), teleoperation(real2sim, real2real), real-time system (ROS1 and ROS2), 3D Scene reconstruction. Embeded Computer Platforms I worked with: Jetson Orin/Xavier, NUC, iOS(Iphone & VisionPro) Robot Platforms I worked with: Boston Dynamic Spot, Custom RC Car, Custom Quadrotors, Franka Panda, Mobile Aloha, UR5. Simulation Enviroments I used: PyBullet, Sapien, IsaacGym/IsaacLab, Mujoco, and Genesis

My research focuses on mobile manipulation, reinforcement learning and control, and tactile sensing. Feel free to reach out for collaboration and discussion of research ideas.
